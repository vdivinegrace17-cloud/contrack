How to Use the Floor Plan Builder
  1. Pick a tool from the left sidebar — Booth, Stall, Table, Kiosk, or a landmark type.
  2. Click a single cell to place a 1×1 item, or click and drag across multiple cells to draw a larger area.
  3. Fill in the details in the popup (label, price, category, status) and click Save.
  4. Switch to Select (or press Esc) to drag items around. Click any item to edit it.
  5. Right-click any item for a quick Edit / Delete menu.
  6. Press Ctrl+Z or click Undo to reverse the last action (up to 50 steps).
  7. Change Cols / Rows and click Update Grid to resize the canvas.
  8. Click Save Layout (or Ctrl+S) when done. The yellow Unsaved badge disappears on success.
